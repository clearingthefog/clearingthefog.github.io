WAR Leaders Chart

For each player, their three WAR estimates are shown as vertical bars (Baseball Reference, FanGraphs, and Baseball Prospectus respectively), with the average as the horizontal line. Notice how the spread between WAR estimates tends to be much larger for speed-and-defense players than pure sluggers.

Most plot elements will tell you more information if you hover over them, and the legend is clickable to toggle visibility for groups of items. Use the view-slider at the bottom to horizontally expand, contract, or slide.
